ABOUT US

YUL is all about living life to the fullest during our favorite season of the year, winter. We realize many others dread the thought of winter, snow, cold, so we set out to change that mindset. When you travel with us, you will have the option of choosing your own path of excitement. Simply want to enjoy the breathtaking landscape? Be our guest. Want to step it up a notch and travel to the tops of mountains? We're ready to go! A getaway with YUL is unlike anything you have experienced before and we take pride in knowing that our locations offer a glimpse of the world that most have never even ventured to explore.

DESTINATIONS

We annually scour the planet looking for specific destinations we know will bring the full effect of the winter spirit. With each year that passes the goal remains the same, always be unique!

THE CABIN

Perfectly positioned in the middle of Lake Moraine's camp grounds, this tranquil getaway is the ultimate place to recharge. Nestle in with a good book and enjoy the rustic log cabin feel along the water's edge. No WIFI, no neighbors, no stress. Just you, your thoughts, and mother nature. Some call it boring, others call it life as it should be. Can you handle it?

THE SLOPES

Pack your skis and head out to the Swiss Alps with this getaway. Located in Zermatt, the Matterhorn is open for adventure 365 days a year. We've worked closely with staff and experts at Zermatt to provide the safest course for any level. Our experts will meet you at the base and coordinate all lift access, drop in points and will also recommend the best locations to stop and take in the sights.

THE POINT

Ever felt the crisp air at 5,000+ feet? How about pushed your physical and mental limits to their max just before reaching the summit? Now is your chance to take in the world from an entirely new perspective. Travel along sign our seasoned guides and camp out amongst the stars on this five day trek to the point.

START PACKING

By now you might be starting to see just why we love the winter season so much. What do you say we start planning that trip? Our dedicated agents are ready and waiting.
